Bio

I am Cinque Mason 19 from Denver, Colorado USA and currently in college. I am in college as a communications major with Anthropology minor and International development minors. I have traveled to parts of the world in search of finding myself and helping others. I have been to Central America/Caribbean (Costa Rica, Panama, Mexico, Turks and Caicos) North America (United States, Canada) Africa(Ethiopia, Kenya) Asia (India, Japan). With plans to go to 14 countries between 2020-2021 some of the countries being in the Middle East and Europe. I traveled as a kid with family but now have been traveling in search to learn doing research in india and will be doing school in the middle east and europe.

I have turned my focus in life to serving people with a focus in my life to work with Refugees from Natural calamities, domestic abuse and war conflicts With this website and my social outreach I have the hopes to get people more involved with humanity.
